Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 400g tomato sauce
  • lentils
  • rosemary
  • oregano
  • basil
  • lasagna sheets
  • mozzarella

Bechamel sauce

  • 500 ml milk
  • 2 tsp flour
  • salt, pepper, nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Make the sauce
  2. layer sauce and lasagna sheets
  3. sprinkle with cheese
  4. add bechamel
  5. bake in oven at 200C for 30 min
