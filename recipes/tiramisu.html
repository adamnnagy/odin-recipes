Tiramisu

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 500g mascarpone
  • 1 cup of coffee
  • 3 tsp brown sugar
  • 3 tsp white sugar

Instructions

  1. whisk together egg yolk and the sugars
  2. add mascarpone to the mix
  3. add brown rum or amaretto
  4. whisk egg white and add to mix
  5. layer coffee dipped ladyfingers with mascarpone cream into bowl
  6. sprinkle with cocoa powder
